# ADR-001: Pre-Copa Bet Modal — 3-Step Fullscreen Modal Flow

## Status

Accepted

## Date

2026-05-24

## Context

When a user enters a league for the first time, the product needs to collect their champion and vice-champion predictions before the Copa do Mundo 2026 kicks off. These bets are worth significant points (+50 for champion, +25 for vice) and are locked after June 11, 2026. The collection UX must be compelling, clear, and visually faithful to the reference design. Three structural approaches were evaluated.

## Decision

Implement a 3-step fullscreen modal that appears sequentially after the existing 4-step LeagueWelcomeModal completes. Steps: (1) champion selection, (2) vice-champion selection with the champion greyed out, (3) summary confirmation with deadline notice. The modal uses official SVG flags, a scrollable card (12 featured teams + all 32 accessible below), and a 3-dash progress indicator.

## Alternatives Considered

### Alternative 1: 2-Step Modal (Combined Selection)

- **Description**: Champion and vice selection on the same screen, confirm on step 2.
- **Pros**: Fewer steps, faster completion.
- **Cons**: Screen becomes crowded with 64 slots; loses the "one decision at a time" psychological clarity that increases completion rates.
- **Why rejected**: Design reference explicitly separates champion and vice into distinct steps. Combining them reduces focus and increases cognitive load.

### Alternative 2: Inline Page (`/ligas/[id]/aposta`)

- **Description**: Redirect user to a dedicated route instead of using a modal.
- **Pros**: Native navigation feel, better deep-linking, no modal stacking.
- **Cons**: Navigation-heavy; breaks the "open league, immediately prompted" feel. Design reference is modal-based. Adds route complexity.
- **Why rejected**: The desired UX is an immediate prompt on league entry, which modals handle better than route redirects.

## Consequences

### Positive

- Exact fidelity to the reference design.
- Sequential flow after welcome modal gives users context (they just learned about scoring) before being asked to bet.
- Per-league scoping allows users to have different predictions in different leagues.
- Editable until Copa kick-off maintains engagement without frustration.

### Negative

- Users must complete two sequential modals on first league access (welcome + bet). Combined, this is 7 steps — may feel lengthy.
- Modal stacking requires careful state management.

### Risks

- Users may abandon after the welcome modal before seeing the bet modal. Mitigation: show bet modal on any subsequent visit if bet hasn't been placed yet (not just first visit).
