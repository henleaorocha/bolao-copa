# ADR-003: Seed 020 is the source of truth for the 2026 team roster

## Status

Accepted

## Date

2026-06-01

## Context

Two team lists disagree in the codebase. `lib/copa-teams.ts` (which drives team-name validity, flags, and the champion bet) lists teams that include **Italy**, which did not qualify for 2026 in reality. The group-stage seed migration (`...020_seed_real_copa2026_group_stage.sql`, annotated "verified May 2026" from FIFA/NBC sources) carries the **real 48-team draw**. The two differ by six teams each way: the seed adds Iran, Iraq, Sweden, Turkey, Bosnia & Herzegovina, and DR Congo; it omits Italy, Cameroon, Denmark, Honduras, Jamaica, and Bolivia. Because team-name validity gates knockout confirmation and champion bets, a wrong roster blocks predictions for real teams.

## Decision

Treat the **seed 020 roster as the source of truth**. Reconcile `lib/copa-teams.ts` (and everything derived from it — name validity, flags, the champion/runner-up picker) to match the real 48 teams. The owner confirmed seed 020 can be trusted.

## Alternatives Considered

### Alternative 1: Keep copa-teams.ts as-is

- **Description**: Leave the existing list unchanged.
- **Pros**: No change.
- **Cons**: Contains a non-qualified team (Italy) and is missing real qualifiers; blocks valid predictions and champion bets.
- **Why rejected**: Demonstrably wrong against the real draw.

### Alternative 2: Owner hand-curates a fresh list before any change

- **Description**: Wait for the owner to supply/validate a definitive list.
- **Pros**: Maximum certainty.
- **Cons**: Adds a manual gate; seed 020 is already the verified real draw.
- **Why rejected**: Owner accepted seed 020 as authoritative, so the extra step is unnecessary.

## Consequences

### Positive

- Team validity, flags, and champion bets become consistent with the real tournament and with the openfootball data.

### Negative

- Touches a widely-used module and its consumers/tests; requires careful reconciliation.

### Risks

- Seed 020 itself could contain an error → mitigation: cross-check against the openfootball 2026 team list during implementation.
